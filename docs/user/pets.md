# Pets

Pets are an **optional collection game**. Your training earns XP, XP grows
pixel-art animals from babies into jacked beasts, and staying consistent earns
eggs that add new species to your collection. It's purely for fun — it has zero
effect on your workouts, records, or any of your training data, and you can turn
it off any time.

## Getting started

Tick **"Hatch a workout buddy?"** during first-time setup, or turn the game on
later in **Settings → Pet game**. Either way you start with **one egg** to
hatch.

When you opt in, your past training counts: existing achievements pay a
one-time retroactive credit (up to enough for one full evolution), so if you've
been training a while your first pet may hatch already grown.

## How pets earn XP

Only your **active pet** earns XP — the one marked "Active" in your collection.
Switching who's active is free and instant, so you can always raise whoever you
like. XP comes from two places:

| Source | XP |
| --- | --- |
| Completing a workout | 40 (the same whether or not you logged stats) |
| Earning an achievement | 15–40, depending on how hard it is |

Every workout and achievement pays out **once**, so editing or re-syncing old
workouts never changes your totals.

## Evolution

Each pet grows through four stages as it earns XP:

| Stage | Total XP |
| --- | --- |
| Baby | 0 |
| Juvenile | 250 |
| Adult | 550 |
| Jacked | 1000 |

The gaps get bigger each time, so the final **Jacked** form feels earned. When
your active pet evolves at the end of a workout, you'll see it celebrated in the
workout summary.

## Eggs & your collection

You earn a new egg **every 5 workouts**. Tap an egg on the Pets page to hatch it
— it's the fun part, so it's never automatic. By default each egg hatches a
species you don't own yet, with no duplicates.

- **Name it** — type your own or hit **🎲 Roll** for a suggestion.
- **Duplicates** — when hatching, you can tick **"Allow duplicate species"** if
  you'd rather keep collecting animals you already own. It's off by default.
  Once you've collected all 14 species, duplicates turn on automatically so your
  eggs keep hatching.

## Your pet's overview

Tap any pet in your collection to open its overview:

- **Its earlier forms** — click through baby, juvenile, adult, and jacked;
  stages it hasn't reached yet are locked.
- **When it grew** — the date it reached each stage.
- **Age vs. time active** — how long ago it hatched, and the total time it's
  spent as your active pet (added up across every stint, even if you switched
  away and back).
- **What it trains** — the three body parts most of its XP came from. Raise a
  pet on leg day and it'll proudly say it trains quads.

## Roughly how it's paced

A new lifter training three times a week fully evolves their first pet in about
**4–6 weeks** — right when a new habit needs the encouragement. Eggs arrive at
about the rate one pet matures, so your collection grows alongside your
training, and collecting all 14 species is a long-tail goal of roughly five
months. The numbers are deliberately generous: this is a reward for showing up,
not another target to hit.

## Turning it off

**Settings → Pet game** disables everything — pets stop appearing across the
app, and nothing is deleted. While it's off, the XP you'd have earned is kept
aside ("banked"). When you turn it back on, you choose whether to spend those
banked points on your active pet or start fresh. Your collection and each pet's
XP are always safe either way.
